COVID-19 Social Mobility Results

Twitter Social Mobility Index, a measure of social distancing and travel, is derived from Twitter data.
Geolocation data from 50 states, 2 territories, 100 most populated cities in the US were surveyed. We also provide an average outlook at the US as a whole and its 6 regions (Northeast, Midwest, South, Central, West, Caribbean).
